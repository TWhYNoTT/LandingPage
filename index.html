Employment and Freelancing Forum

In its second session

MAY 2024

About the Event

The Information Technology Institute at Assiut University is pleased to announce the second edition of the Employment and Freelancing Fair.

This major event will feature professional talks by industry experts and company representatives, specialized workshops, and panel discussions covering various technological topics. Attendees will hear success stories from distinguished graduates and see displays of outstanding graduation projects and freelance work by students and trainees. Innovative and creative ideas from students will also be presented. The fair will facilitate networking with IT companies and provide job and training opportunities. Open to students and graduates, this fair is a unique opportunity to gain industry insights, explore career options, and enhance professional skills. Don’t miss the chance to participate and make valuable connections. Register now and join us for this significant event.

KEY HIGHLIGHTS

The fair will feature a variety of activities designed to provide valuable insights and opportunities for attendees.

Professional Talks

These sessions will feature experts in the field of technology as well as representatives from leading companies. They will deliver professional talks on a wide range of technological topics, providing attendees with the latest industry trends and insights.

Partnership

The fair will facilitate partnerships with IT companies, enhancing cooperation between academia and the industry. Representatives from various companies will be present, providing attendees with networking opportunities.

Success Stories

The fair will showcase the success stories of graduates who have excelled in the field of IT. These stories aim to inspire current students and recent graduates by highlighting the achievements of their predecessors.

Available Jobs

One of the key features of the fair is the provision of job and training opportunities. Attendees will have the chance to explore various employment and internship options, aiding their transition from academia to the professional world.
